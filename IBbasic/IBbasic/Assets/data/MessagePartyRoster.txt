<yl>PARTY ROSTER</yl>
<gn>Manage Your Party</gn>
<bu>1. Party Size:</bu> At the top of the screen, there will be a message defining the maximum number of player created characters that you can add to your party at this time (defined by the module's author)
<bu>2. Down Arrow:</bu> pressing this button will remove the currently selected PC from the party and into the PC roster list.
<bu>3. Up Arrow:</bu> pressing this button will remove the currently selected PC from the PC roster and add them to the party.
